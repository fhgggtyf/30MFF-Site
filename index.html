Zephyr

Walking in the depth of hell, you are struggling to survive. You can feel the pressure in the air, the demons whispering in your head telling you to give up. But you can't, you have too much that you care about. You tell yourself it will all be over soon, but you are almost at your limits. The things you care are weighing you down, but you keep telling yourself: Keep moving... Keep moving...

During your wandering, you notice strange things happening all around you. As you move on, continuously pushing your limits, your thoughts starts flying. You can't help but think about what got you here. It felt so unreal. Now what happened before felt more like a dream, the only truths left are pain and misery...

Genre: 2D rougue-like

Game Duration: 30min/run

Game Engine: Unity

GitHub: https://github.com/fhgggtyf/Zephyr
